Guidance for your service

You must follow all relevant standards to gain assurance for your service.

These JSP 604 rules explain how to deliver through life management in your service.

  1. Rule 14

    ICT shall be developed and its performance demonstrated with respect to the environment in which it will operate

  2. Rule 15

    ICT shall have suitable representative test environments (RTE) available to enable through life testing to be conducted

  3. Rule 16

    ICT capabilities and services shall be designed to provide a high-quality product, meeting the customers' requirements for reliability, maintainability, usability and serviceability thereby permitting holistic service management

  4. Rule 17

    ICT shall have defined support arrangements

  5. Rule 18

    ICT shall have appropriate software licenses procured and managed through life

  6. Rule 19

    ICT shall comply with spectrum management requirements

  7. Rule 20

    ICT shall comply with electromagnetic integration requirements

  8. Rule 21

    ICT shall be compliant with safety and environmental requirements

  9. Rule 22

    ICT shall be compliant with HMG ia policy when employing cryptographic products
