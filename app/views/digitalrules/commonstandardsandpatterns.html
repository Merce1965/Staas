Rule 1 - Make use of common standards and patterns

To guide, support, and cohere technology solutions in the best interests of Defence as a whole.

This rule forms part of the Defence Digital Rules, as embodied in the Defence Service Manual.

You must use approved common standards, technical reference architectures and patterns for your project, whenever possible.

This helps us achieve a standard approach to building services so we can work efficiently now and in the future.

Before you can get approval to proceed with your project, you must submit the idea for your project to the Architecture Triage Board.

We will review your idea and explain what you need to do in order to proceed. The review can take up to 4 weeks, so we recommend you submit your idea as early as possible.

What you must do

When developing a new, or changing an existing digital service, you must:

  • submit your project idea to the Architecture Triage Board
  • follow the guidance in our Common Technology Architecture
  • follow the guidance in The Foundry Way (coming soon)
