Rule 7 - Accelerate and innovate

Deliver a competitive edge with emergent and enabling technologies such as AI, IoT and Synthetics.

This rule forms part of the Defence Digital Rules, as embodied in the Defence Service Manual.

To accelerate our strategic edge, we need to support the widespread adoption and scaling up of modern technologies by being aware of wider industry trends and working with our suppliers and partners to ensure we maintain maximum exposure to emerging technologies.

To deliver a competitive edge we encourage the use of suitable emerging and enabling technologies, such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA)
  • Intelligent Automation (IA)
  • Augmented/Virtual Reality (AR/VR)
  • Graph Technologies
  • 3D Printing
  • Biometrics

Robotics Process Automation (RPA) and low code

Innovation isn't just about using the latest cutting-edge technologies, but also applying established enabling technologies in novel ways to current problems, like applying RPA to replace human led data entry into existing applications.

To understand how you can use RPA and low code for your service, contact the Automation team.

What you must do

  • use our MODCloud platform as the default choice when building new digital services
  • use Machine Learning (ML) and AI-based automation to improve decision-making
  • consider how new and existing technologies could be applied in novel ways to solve problems
  • use our common automation platform
  • follow the AI and Automation Centre of Excellence’s standards and guidance
