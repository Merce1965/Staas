Availability Management

ICT shall be designed such that service availability, and therefore its constituent aspects including reliability, mantainability, supportability, usability etc. are integral to the solution design and are considered from the beginning of the service lifecycle through an iterative process of progressive assurance.

For more information see JSP604 Rule 16.6
